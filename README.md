# Euromart-Dashboard
1. Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing EuroMart retail sales data across European countries using Power BI. The goal is to build an interactive dashboard that provides insights into sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, and product trends.

By analyzing key business metrics such as sales, profit, discounts, and shipping modes, the dashboard helps stakeholders make data-driven decisions to improve sales strategies, optimize inventory management, and enhance customer targeting.

2. Tools & Technologies

Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

SQL – Data querying and transformation

Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data cleaning and preprocessing

Matplotlib – Data visualization during exploratory analysis

Excel / CSV Dataset

3. Problem Statement

Retail businesses often struggle to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and regional demand patterns.

This project addresses these challenges by analyzing EuroMart sales data to:

. Evaluate sales performance and profitability

. Identify high-performing and loss-making products

. Analyze the impact of discounts on profit

. Understand customer purchasing patterns

. Compare regional sales performance across Europe

. Evaluate shipping modes and their effect on sales

4.Dashboard Features

---Sales Performance Analysis

Total Sales, Total Profit, and Quantity KPIs

Sales trend analysis over time

Profitability analysis by product category

---Customer Insights

Customer segmentation by region, country, city, and segment

Analysis of customer feedback and purchasing behavior

---Regional Performance

Geo-based map visualizations of sales across European countries

Identification of top-performing and underperforming regions

---Product Analysis

Sales and profit analysis by category and sub-category

Identification of top-selling and high-profit products

---Shipping Mode Analysis

Comparison of Economy, Economy Plus, and Priority shipping modes

Analysis of shipping methods and their impact on sales and profitability

5.Key Insights

. Certain high discount levels lead to negative profits.

. Office Supplies and Furniture categories contribute significantly to overall sales.

. Sales performance varies across European regions, highlighting potential market opportunities.

. Shipping mode selection influences customer purchasing behavior and profitability.

6. Project Deliverables

. Interactive Power BI Dashboard

. Sales trend and profitability analysis

. Customer segmentation insights

. Regional sales performance visualization

. Product performance analysis
